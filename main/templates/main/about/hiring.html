Join Our Team!

Community Bike Works is looking for enthusiastic and dedicated individuals to join our team. If you are passionate about working with youth and making a difference in the community, we want to hear from you!

Job Description:

  • Teach classes at our Allentown and Easton bike shops, and in Allentown, Bethlehem, and Easton schools.
  • Lead our afterschool and summer Drop-In program by coordinating fun and education activities.
  • Build meaningful relationships with students that offer them mentorship and support.
  • Maintain student records and complete other administrative tasks needed to run programs.
  • Assist with bike repairs, bike donations, and in the upkeep of the Community Bike Works bike shop.
  • Participate in recreational bike rides on local trails.
  • Represent Community Bike Works in the community.

Qualifications:

  • Self-motivated, willing to learn, and able to work both independently and as part of a team.
  • Commitment to the values of Community Bike Works and inclusivity in interactions with students, partners, and staff.
  • Complete state and federal child abuse clearance checks.

  • Comfortable riding a bike; training will be provided if needed.
  • Fluency in Spanish is beneficial but not required.

Hours & Salary:

  • Full-time positions available Monday through Friday, 10 am - 6 pm, with occasional Saturdays as required.
  • Hourly rate: $25.00/hr, with benefits including paid time off, health insurance, and other perks for full-time employees.
  • Part-time positions also available. Rate: $18/hr with similar benefits, including paid time off.
